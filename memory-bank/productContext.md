# Product Context - Nefes Yoga Studio

## Why This Product Exists
Nefes Yoga Studio needs a digital presence that reflects its physical atmosphere - calm, welcoming, and centered. The website serves as both a marketing tool and an extension of the studio's philosophy: breathing, mindfulness, and inner peace.

## Problems It Solves
1. **Discovery**: Potential students can find and learn about the studio
2. **Information**: Class types, schedules, and instructor profiles are easily accessible
3. **Conversion**: Clear CTAs guide visitors to sign up for trial classes
4. **Brand Experience**: The website itself creates a calming experience, differentiating from generic fitness websites

## User Experience Goals
- Visitor feels calm upon landing on the site
- Navigation is intuitive and non-intrusive
- Information is organized in a natural flow (introduction -> classes -> schedule -> people -> contact)
- The breath circle animation sets the tone immediately
- Smooth scrolling creates a meditative browsing experience

## Content Strategy
- Turkish language throughout
- Warm, inviting copy that speaks directly to the visitor
- Emphasis on personal transformation and inner peace
- Professional but approachable tone
