# Project Brief - Nefes Yoga Studio

## Overview
Nefes Yoga Studio is a single-page landing website for a yoga/meditation studio located in Istanbul. The website provides a calming, organic digital experience with earth-tone colors and slow, breath-synchronized animations.

## Goals
- Create a serene, peaceful digital experience that slows the visitor's breath
- Showcase the studio's classes, schedule, instructors, and contact information
- Drive conversions through trial class sign-ups
- Establish brand identity with earth tones, serif typography, and organic movements

## Target Audience
- Istanbul residents interested in yoga and meditation
- People seeking stress relief and mindfulness
- All experience levels from beginners to advanced practitioners

## Key Features
- 7-section scroll experience (Hero, About, Classes, Schedule, Instructors, Testimonials, Contact/Footer)
- Breath circle animation (4-second breathing cycle)
- Smooth scroll with Lenis
- Responsive design (mobile-first)
- Floating label contact form

## Design Principles
- Calm and peaceful atmosphere
- Earth tones: cream, forest green, warm gold
- Serif typography (Cormorant Garamond) for headings
- Sans-serif (Lato) for body text
- Slow animations (minimum 0.8s duration)
- Organic, natural-feeling movements
